What is it about?

Ahoy! presents a groundbreaking iOS game application that hand coded and designed for Board Game enthusiasts. This inventive application takes the classic 'Guess Who?' board game to the next level by embracing AR (Augmented Reality) technology. With Ahoy!, players dive into an imaginative realm where the boundaries between the physical and virtual worlds blur. This app brings to life the timeless charm of 'Guess Who?' by allowing both players to interact with virtual characters seamlessly integrated into their real environment. This dynamic fusion of tradition and innovation elevates the excitement and engagement of gameplay, propelling 'Guess Who?' into the digital era with a touch of nostalgia.
